      Understanding Phishing Attacks

      Suspicious URLs

      Phishing URLs often mimic legitimate sites with slight spelling variations, excessive subdomains, or suspicious domains.

      Urgency Tactics

      Phishers create artificial urgency ("Your account will be closed!") to bypass rational decision-making.

      Brand Impersonation

      Attackers impersonate trusted brands like PayPal, Amazon, or your bank to gain credibility.

      Credential Harvesting

      The ultimate goal is usually to steal login credentials, financial information, or personal data.
